Samba OG Shoe

Description

The Adidas Samba, originally designed as a football trainer, has evolved into a streetwear icon. With its timeless three-stripe design, it remains a globally recognized and stylish staple. Comfortable and versatile, it's perfect for everyday wear. Whether dressing up or down, the Samba adds a touch of effortless cool to any outfit.

Design and Style

The Samba OG Shoe has a timeless, simple design, that offers endless styling options. With a simple white base and black stripes, the shoe features a sleek, minimalist design rooted in Adidas' heritage. Its smooth leather upper contrasts with suede overlays, while the iconic three black stripes add bold definition. A classic gum sole enhances traction and vintage appeal, making it a classic streetwear staple.
